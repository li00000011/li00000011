Conference Papers

High-Precision Mixed Feature Fusion Network Using Hypergraph Computation for Cervical Abnormal Cell Detection

Conference: MICCAI 2025

Publication Date: 2025.06

Authors: Jincheng Li, Danyang Dong, Menglin Zheng, Jingbo Zhang, Yueqin Hang, Lichi Zhang, and Lili Zhao

Highlights: A hypergraph-enhanced fusion network that jointly models inter-cell spatial correlations and intra-cell discriminative features achieves state-of-the-art cervical abnormal cell detection on three public datasets.

Journal Papers

PC-CS-YOLO: High-Precision Obstacle Detection for Visually Impaired Safety

Journal: Sensors

Publication Date: 2025.01

Authors: Jincheng Li, Menglin Zheng, Danyang Dong and Xing Xie

Highlights: PC-CS-YOLO boosts obstacle detection for the visually impaired by fusing partial convolutions with cross-scale attention, outperforming YOLO11 in precision, recall, and mAP while cutting inference time to 20.6 ms.
